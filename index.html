BRIEF INTRODUCTION of CONE Studio
CONE Studio is a group of individuals who share a common passion for photography, established at Vo Nguyen Giap High School in 2022. The studio consists of five talented members: Tran Hai Thao Quang, Pham Nguyen Quoc Huy, Le Minh Huy, Pham Thanh Lam and Nguyen Trung Tin. All current members are former students of Vo Nguyen Giap High School (graduating class of 2019-2022). CONE Studio was founded with a deep passion for photography and the art of visual storytelling. We believe that photography has the ability to capture the unique beauty of the world around us, be it humans, animals, flowers, or landscapes, and we aspire to share this passion with others. By combining our talents, creative abilities, and technical skills, we have created distinctive and visually stunning photographic artworks. Our goal is to capture breathtaking images that evoke emotions and tell stories through our lenses. With an unwavering spirit of creativity, we are committed to providing our clients with engaging and one-of-a-kind experiences. Join us on a journey to explore the world through our lenses and discover the hidden beauty in every moment!
